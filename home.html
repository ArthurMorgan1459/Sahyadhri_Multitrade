ABOUT US

Established in 1983, Sahyadhri Multitrade Pvt Ltd began with a mission to provide time-sensitive express cargo services across 6 initial branches in India. With an excellent service record spanning over 30 years, we are a partner you can rely on with complete trust and faith.

While our foundation is built on leadership in freight forwarding and transportation, we have successfully expanded into the Scrap Industry. Today, we offer comprehensive supply chain solutions, including:

  • Express GTA, PTL, and FTL services.
  • Integrated Scrap Supply Chain management.
  • Reliable logistics for businesses expanding in both revenue and volume.

SERVICES

With over 30 years of expertise, Sahyadhri Multitrade Pvt Ltd provides end-to-end management for the scrap industry. We specialize in transforming industrial waste into valuable resources through efficient, time-sensitive logistics.

  • Integrated Scrap Supply Chain: Expert management of GTA, PTL, and FTL movements specifically for scrap materials.
  • Professional Sorting & Grading: Systematic separation of ferrous, non-ferrous, and TMT scrap to ensure high-quality standards.
  • Advanced Processing: Material preparation including cleaning and shredding to support sustainable manufacturing.
