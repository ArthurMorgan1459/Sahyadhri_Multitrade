SERVICES

Sahyadhri Multitrade Pvt Ltd provides integrated scrap supply chain and logistics solutions, including transportation (FTL/PTL), material sorting, grading, and processing. We ensure smooth handling and efficient conversion of scrap into reusable resources through a well-managed supply chain.

Integrated Scrap Supply Chain Solutions

Scrap Supply Chain & Logistics

Efficient management of GTA, PTL, and FTL movements ensures timely and reliable transportation of scrap materials across diverse locations, supporting smooth industrial operations.

Sorting & Grading

We systematically separate ferrous, non-ferrous, and TMT scrap materials based on type and quality, ensuring high standards and optimal usability for further processing.

Processing & Material Preparation

Our advanced processing methods include cleaning, shredding, and preparing materials for reuse, helping transform industrial scrap into valuable resources for manufacturing.
