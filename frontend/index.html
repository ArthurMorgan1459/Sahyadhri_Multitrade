Who We Are

ABOUT US

Sahyadhri Multitrade Pvt Ltd is a professionally driven organisation specialising in the scrap industry. We deliver end-to-end solutions for the sourcing, handling, grading, and processing of ferrous and non-ferrous scrap materials across India.

Though a young company, our team brings deep industry expertise and strong trade networks — enabling us to serve some of India's leading steel manufacturers and industrial enterprises with consistent quality and on-time delivery.

  • Sourcing and trading of ferrous and non-ferrous scrap materials.
  • Professional sorting, grading, and processing of scrap.
  • Integrated scrap supply chain management across India.
